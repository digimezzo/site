# Dopamine 3.0.4

Hello everyone!

I’m excited to bring you **Dopamine 3.0.4**, a small but handy update that refines your listening experience and fixes a few pesky issues. This release focuses on improving usability and polishing some visual features, so you can enjoy your music without distractions.

One of the highlights in 3.0.4 is the ability to **enlarge the cover image on the Now Playing screen**. Whether you love seeing album art big and bold or just want a more immersive experience, this feature makes it easy. On top of that, **clicking the visualization now switches it between the available styles**, giving you more control over your musical ambiance.

As always, we’ve also fixed a bunch of issues to make your music experience smoother, from playback glitches to playlist management and NAS browsing.

Here’s the full change log:

### Added

- Added ability to enlarge the cover image on the Now Playing screen
- Clicking the visualization switches it between the available styles

### Changed

- Updated the Portuguese (Brazilian) translation
- Updated the Swedish translation

### Fixed

- Delete button is not visible when folder is not selected in "Manage collection" screen
- Window size increases when dragging
- Attempting to delete a song from a smart playlist breaks the playlist
- Deleting a song from a playlist while filtering via search removes most of the files from the playlist
- Playback progress bar does not react to touch input
- The cover image on the Now Playing screen has glitches
- Browsing folders on a NAS gets stuck in an infinite loop
- Follow the system color does not work on Linux

This update is all about making your music look and feel better, while keeping your library management smooth and reliable. I hope you enjoy the improvements!
