Dopamine 3.0.3

Hello all!

I'm excited to share Dopamine 3.0.3, a feature-packed update that adds some of the most requested listening and library features so far. This release focuses on giving you more control over how your music plays and looks, while also improving reliability across the app.

One of the highlights of this version is the introduction of crossfade, which lets songs blend smoothly into each other for a more seamless listening experience. Alongside that, smart playlists arrive in Dopamine, making it easier to automatically organize and rediscover music in your collection.

Another big improvement is how easy it is to start listening right away. You can now shuffle directly from artist, genre, and album menus, or simply use Shuffle All from key library screens to instantly jump into your music.

This update also brings beautiful new themes: Catppuccin and Dracula (thanks to BlackSpirits), giving Dopamine a fresh look for anyone who enjoys customizing their player.

And for mini player fans, there are new Always on top and Lock position options, making it easier to keep your music controls exactly where you want them.

This is the change log:

### Added

- Added shuffle option to the artist, genre and album context menus.
- Added shuffle all on artist, genre, album and songs screens.
- Added crossfade
- Added smart playlists
- Added Catppuccin and Dracula themes (Thank you BlackSpirits!)
- Added "Always on top" and "Lock position" options to the mini player context menu

### Changed

- Updated the Croatian translation
- Updated the German translation
- Updated the Italian translation
- Updated the Portuguese (brazilian) translation
- Updated the Portuguese (Portugal) translation
- Updated the Simplified Chinese translation
- Updated the Spanish translation
- Updated the Vietnamese translation

### Fixed

- Genres which are starting with numbers are incorrectly detected
- Play and skip count are not updated when collection songs are played from playlists
- Flac rating is lost after editing tags
- Dopamine window doesn't show when clicking on pinned Taskbar icon in Windows
- Error when deleting songs that are on a network drive
- The titlebar is only draggable in empty spots

This update adds a lot of polish and some powerful new ways to enjoy your music. I hope you like it as much as I do!

Enjoy the music!
