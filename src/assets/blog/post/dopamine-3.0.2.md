# Dopamine 3.0.2

Hello all!

I'm happy to announce the release of Dopamine 3.0.2, a maintenance update that brings a few nice enhancements, updated translations, and important bug fixes to improve your listening experience.

This release introduces a brand-new Highlights screen, giving you an at-a-glance view of your most played albums, a great way to rediscover your favorites.
The tag editor has also been expanded with support for Composers, Conductor, and BPM, making Dopamine even better for managing music libraries.

### Added

- Added a Highlights screen showing your most played albums
- Added Composers, Conductor and BPM to tag editor.

### Changed

- Updated the Turkish translation
- Updated the Vietnamese translation

### Fixed

- Fixed a crash that occurred when playing a song from the playback queue
- Fixed some album view issues
- Fixed an issue causing the wrong song to play when started from the Operating System

Enjoy the music!
